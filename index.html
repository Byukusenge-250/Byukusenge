🧠 What is Every AI?

Every AI is a modern platform created to help users easily discover, compare, and choose the best Artificial Intelligence tools available today — covering image editing, video creation, music generation, and chat assistants in one simple place.

🎨 Image AI Tools

Platforms like Midjourney, DALL·E, and Leonardo AI allow users to generate high-quality images from text prompts, edit photos, create digital art, and design professional visuals without advanced skills.

🎬 Video AI Tools

Tools such as Runway, Pika Labs, and Synthesia use AI to generate videos from text, apply smart effects, remove backgrounds, and automate video editing for creators and businesses at scale.

🎵 Music & Audio AI

Platforms like Suno, AIVA, and ElevenLabs generate full songs, orchestral scores, and ultra-realistic voices in different styles within seconds — perfect for creators who need copyright-safe audio.

💬 Chat & Productivity AI

ChatGPT, Claude, and Gemini help with writing, coding, research, and daily tasks. Used by students, developers, and businesses worldwide to dramatically boost productivity and creativity.
